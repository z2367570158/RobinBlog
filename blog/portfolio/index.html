  • Dead mine

    • "Dead mine" is a narrative game that player try to save people in a mine leaking toxic gas. At the end of the game, as player going deeper to the mine, even he realize that maybe he is not able to get back but he still keeps working on closing valves to prevent gas leaking.

  • Election Day

    2017-10
    • "Election Day" is an ironic game that player is an election manager and trying to get enough voters to make his c be elected. Player needs to answer voters random ridiculous or reasonable questions. Changing voters' attitude to you and your candidate, making them love you or just bribing them, is all your choice.

  • Jump Builder

    2017-09
    • "Jump Builder" is a game that player can move, jump and most important, build cubes attached with environment. At the start of the game, player can only build one cube. All his mission is to solve puzzles, get more cubes and finally get to the higher place.
